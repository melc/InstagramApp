About ClapPaws
This is official site for ClapPaws. ClapPaws helps dog walkers and dog owners build up business relationship to benefit with each other. ClapPaws's goal is to help dog owners make a NO WORRY NO FEAR HAPPY life with their beloved dog kids. Dog walkers joining ClapPaws will provide their outstanding professional yet humanity skills to make dog kids a wonderful time.
Join ClapPaws
Join our big family and enjoy your time with our members including dog walkers and owners. Visit our website to share your stories and pictures with ClapPaws family members.
